About Landmarks of India

India is also famous for its beautiful artistic and architectural heritage. Iconic monuments such as the Taj Mahal, the Hindu temples of Varanasi, the stone temples of Khajuraho, and the ancient city of Hampi are all testament to the beauty and craftsmanship of Indian architecture.

India is home of the some awe-inspiring landmarks in the world. These monuments reflect its rich cultural heritage and historical significance.

Famous Landmarks in India

Iconic Monuments

The majestic Red Fort in Delhi

  • Taj Mahal : Located in Agra.This symbol of eternal love as one of the seven wonders of the world.

  • Red Fort : A UNESCO World heritage site in Delhi.showcasing Mughal architecture

  • Hawa Mahal : Known as the "palace of winds,"this Jaipur marvel is famous for its intricate latticework.

  • Charminar : An iconic mosque in Hyderabad and a masterpiece of Indo-Islamic architecture.
