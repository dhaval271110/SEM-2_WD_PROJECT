Festivals

India celebrates the variety of festivals that reflect its rich culture heritage and religion diversity

  • Diwali:Festival of lights,symbolizing the victory of light over darkness.
  • Holi:Festival of colors celebrating love and arrival of spring.
  • Eid:Festival of Harmonny. marked by prayers,feasts and charity.
  • Christmas:Festival of joy.celebrating the birth of jesus christ.

Traditional Attire

indian traditional clothing varies by region.Showcasting its culture diversity.

  • Saree:Elegant drape worn by women across India.
  • Kurta-Pajama:Confertable attire for men.popular in the north.
  • Lehnga:A festive outfit of women.especially in weddings.
  • Dhoti:Traditional men's wear in southern and easten India.
