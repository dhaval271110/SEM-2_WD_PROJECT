About culture of India

The beauty of Indian culture lies in its history, and is reflected in its arts, traditions, and spirituality. The arts, including music, dance, and visual arts, play a central role in Indian culture. India is well known for its 'Unity in Diversity' across the world.

FESTIVALS


Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is a major Hindu festival that celebrates the triumph of good over evil. It is celebrated by lighting lamps, bursting crackers, and decorating homes with diyas and rangolis.

LANGUAGES


India is one of the most linguistically diverse countries in the world, with hundreds of languages and dialects spoken across the country.
